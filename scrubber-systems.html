About Product

Industrial Wet Scrubber Systems

Scrubber systems use liquid contact — typically water or a chemical reagent — to absorb, neutralize, or capture pollutants from gas streams. Contaminated exhaust passes through a packed tower or venturi section where intimate gas-liquid contact removes acids, alkalis, soluble gases, and fine particulates. Our scrubbers are fabricated in mild steel with powder-coated finish, configured for automatic operation with recirculation pumps, mist eliminators, and pH monitoring where required. Sized for your gas flow rate, pollutant concentration, and target removal efficiency.
