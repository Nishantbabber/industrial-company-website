Product Overview

Precision Flour Shifting for Industrial Milling

The HarvestHubs flour shifter uses a cylindrical rotating screen to gently agitate milled flour, breaking agglomerates and passing only particles below the selected mesh size. Installed after roller mills or pin mills, it ensures consistent flour texture for atta, maida and specialty grades. The direct-drive motor and food-grade screen materials meet hygiene requirements in commercial flour mills, with dust-tight housing compatible with pneumatic conveying and bagging systems.

Applications

Flour Processing Applications

Final refining step before flour enters storage silos, bulk loaders or retail packaging lines.

Wheat Flour & Atta Mills

Removes roller mill agglomerates and bran specks from whole wheat atta before silo storage and 5–50 kg bag filling.

Bakery & Industrial Baking

Produces lump-free maida and refined flour with uniform particle size for consistent dough hydration and mixing.

Specialty & Multigrain Flour

Screens blended multigrain and pulse flours to a defined mesh, ensuring even distribution of coarse and fine fractions.
