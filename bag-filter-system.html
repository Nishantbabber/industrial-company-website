About Product

Industrial Bag Filter Systems

Bag filter systems use fabric filter bags housed in a sealed chamber to capture fine dust and particulates from process air. Dirty air enters the collector, dust collects on the bag surface, and a pulse-jet cleaning system periodically dislodges accumulated material into a hopper for discharge. Our baghouses are fabricated in mild steel with color-coated finish, configured for continuous-duty operation in cement, feed, metal, and general manufacturing environments.
