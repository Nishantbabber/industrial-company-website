Product Overview

Precision Bulk Transfer for Industrial Grain Processing

The HarvestHubs screw conveyor uses a rotating helical flight inside a trough or tube to move dal, rice, grain flour and compound feed between machines at controlled rates. Available in U-trough and tubular designs for horizontal, inclined and vertical applications, each unit is built to your required length, diameter and capacity. Enclosed construction minimises dust emission, while bolted end plates and removable trough covers simplify flight inspection and maintenance.

Key Advantages

Flexible Conveying Built to Your Layout

Trough type, screw pitch and drive location configured for your material characteristics and plant geometry.

Custom Length & Capacity

Built in lengths from 2 m to 25 m with screw diameters from 200 mm to 600 mm for capacities up to 50 TPH.

Incline Conveying Option

U-trough designs handle inclines up to 30° for applications where bucket elevators are not required.

Enclosed Dust-Tight Design

Cover plates and tubular options contain fine flour and feed dust, supporting cleaner plant environments.

Variable Pitch Flights

Short pitch sections at the inlet prevent flooding, while standard pitch flights maintain uniform discharge along the trough length.
