About Product

Industrial Centrifugal Blowers

Centrifugal blowers generate the airflow and static pressure required to move air through ductwork, bag filters, cyclones, and scrubber systems. Our blowers feature mild steel housings with color-coated finish, dynamically balanced impellers, and electric motor drive at 50 Hz. They serve as the primary air-moving component in dust extraction lines, ventilation systems, and pneumatic material transfer applications across manufacturing and processing plants.
