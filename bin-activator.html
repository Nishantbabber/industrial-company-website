Product Overview

Precision Bin Discharge for Industrial Bulk Storage

The HarvestHubs bin activator mounts beneath silo cone outlets, using gyratory vibration to fluidize stored material and eliminate dead zones caused by bridging or ratholing. Adjustable vibration intensity and outlet diameter suit materials from fine flour and cement powder to plastic pellets and compound feed. Robust steel fabrication with replaceable wear liners handles abrasive products, while the integrated discharge flange connects directly to screw conveyors, rotary valves or pneumatic pickup points.
