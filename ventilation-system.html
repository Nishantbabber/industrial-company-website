About Product

Industrial Ventilation Systems

Industrial ventilation systems control airflow within manufacturing and processing facilities — supplying fresh air, exhausting contaminated or heated air, and maintaining acceptable temperature and contaminant levels. Our systems combine centrifugal or axial fans, galvanized or mild steel ductwork, dampers, and optional filtration or scrubbing stages. Designed as general exhaust, localized process ventilation, or make-up air systems, each installation is sized for the required air changes per hour, static pressure, and building layout.
