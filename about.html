Company introduction

Who we are and what we manufacture.

HarvestHubs Equipments Pvt. Ltd. is an industrial equipment manufacturer based in Pune, Maharashtra. We produce complete pellet and mash feed plants, individual grain processing machines — including precleaners, destoners, hammer mills, classifiers and conveyors — and a full range of pollution control systems such as bag filters, cyclone separators, scrubbers, dust and fume extraction units. We also provide custom fabrication and material handling solutions for distillery, ethanol and bulk processing facilities across India.

Our story

Built on practical engineering and manufacturing discipline.

HarvestHubs was established to address a clear need in the Indian feed and processing industry: reliable, well-engineered equipment backed by responsive technical support. From our first installations in grain handling and feed milling, we expanded into turnkey pellet plant projects and pollution control systems for industrial facilities. Today we serve feed manufacturers, distillery operators, grain processors and industrial plants — delivering equipment that meets operational requirements, not just catalogue specifications.

Our mission

To manufacture and deliver industrial processing equipment that improves plant efficiency, product quality and operational reliability — through disciplined engineering, quality fabrication and responsive client support from project inquiry through after-sales service.

Our vision

To be a trusted manufacturing partner for feed, distillery and industrial processing plants across India — recognized for engineering integrity, equipment durability and long-term client relationships built on consistent project delivery.
