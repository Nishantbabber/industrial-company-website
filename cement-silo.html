About Product

Industrial Cement Storage Silos

Cement silos provide enclosed bulk storage for powdered cement and similar construction materials. Fabricated in mild steel with color-coated surface treatment, our silos feature conical discharge sections, access platforms, and inlet/outlet connections compatible with pneumatic blowers and rotary airlock valves. Available in capacities from 50 to 100 tons, each unit is designed for stable installation, minimal dust escape, and continuous material flow to batching and mixing operations.
