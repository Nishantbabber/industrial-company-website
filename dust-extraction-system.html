About Product

Industrial Dust Extraction Systems

Dust extraction systems capture airborne particulates at the source and transport them through ductwork to a central collector — typically a cyclone, bag filter, or combination unit — before exhausting cleaned air. Our systems include capture hoods, galvanized or MS ducting, centrifugal blowers, and filtration equipment sized for your dust type, particle size, and airflow requirements. Designed for continuous operation in grinding, milling, conveying, and material processing environments.
