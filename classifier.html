Product Overview

Precision Classification for Industrial Grain Processing

The HarvestHubs classifier uses eccentric-weight vibratory motion to move grain across perforated screen decks, separating oversize, on-size and undersize fractions in a single pass. Self-cleaning screen action prevents blinding on sticky or high-moisture materials such as paddy and maize. Compact modular construction allows drop-in integration between pre-cleaners, destoners and storage bins, with recirculation options for fines recovery in pellet and feed lines.

Applications

Sizing Applications Across Grain Processing

Used wherever uniform kernel size improves milling yield, pellet durability or product grading standards.

Paddy & Rice Grading

Separates broken kernels and immature grains from head rice before whitening, improving whole grain recovery rates.

Pellet Feed Screening

Classifies cooled pellets into on-size product, fines for recirculation and overs for crumbling in animal feed plants.

Maize & Seed Sorting

Removes small and oversized maize kernels before dry milling or seed treatment, ensuring uniform conditioning results.
