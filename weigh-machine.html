Product Overview

Precision Weighing for Industrial Batching & Packaging

HarvestHubs weigh machines combine load cell platforms with digital weighing electronics for accurate ingredient batching in feed formulation and finished product bagging in grain plants. Direct digital communication between the scale indicator and PLC controller automates dosing sequences, records batch weights and transmits production data to SCADA systems for real-time monitoring. Configurable for gross, net and tare weighing, each system is calibrated for your capacity range and material flow rate.
