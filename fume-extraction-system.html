About Product

Industrial Fume Extraction Systems

Fume extraction systems remove hazardous gases, vapors, and fine aerosols generated during welding, chemical processing, soldering, and thermal operations. Capture hoods or arms positioned near the emission source draw contaminated air through ductwork to filtration or scrubbing equipment before discharge. Our systems are fabricated in mild steel with color-coated finish, sized for the required face velocity and airflow to ensure effective contaminant capture at each workstation or process point.
