Product Overview

Precision Pre-Cleaning for Industrial Grain Processing

The HarvestHubs pre-cleaner uses a rotating cylindrical sieve to separate oversize particles, chaff and foreign material from free-flowing grains, malt and feed ingredients. Installed at the intake point of feed mills, rice mills and ethanol plants, it protects hammer mills, destoners and storage systems from blockages and abrasive damage. Low power draw, balanced construction and accessible screen panels keep maintenance straightforward during continuous-duty operation.

Applications

Where Pre-Cleaners Are Deployed

First-stage screening for any facility receiving bulk grain, feed ingredients or malt in truck, rail or silo discharge streams.

Feed & Pellet Mills

Removes straw, cobs and oversize material from maize, soybean meal and compound feed ingredients before grinding and batching.

Rice & Paddy Mills

Separates husk fragments, panicles and trash from paddy and rice intake to improve whitener efficiency and reduce bran contamination.

Ethanol & Distillery Plants

Conditions incoming maize and broken grain by removing stalk pieces and foreign matter before hammer milling and fermentation.
